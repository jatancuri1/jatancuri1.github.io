Victor Cruz

This site is all about Victor Cruz, one of the greatest NFL current and all time wide recievers. We will follow his journey full of hardship to the top. All his life he kept fighting to get to where he is now.








Basic Information on Cruz

  • Born on Nov. 11, 1986
  • Son of Mike Walker and Blanca Cruz
  • Partner: Elaina Watley
  • Has 1 daughter, Kennedy cruz
  • Attended and played football at The University of Massachussets
  • Height:6'0"
  • Weight:204lbs
  • Is currently a Wide reciever for the New York Giants

The Struggle

  • Born in Paterson, New Jersey(a rough neighborhood)
  • His parents were never married
  • Father was gone a lot, he was a firefighter
  • Attended a less known Division 1 college that doesn't recieve much recognition
  • Recieved less attention due to his size(5'9",165 lbs) during highschool
  • Improved test scores at a community college in order to attend college and play football
  • Kicked out of college because his grades went back down
  • Returned back home and live in his mom's house
  • His father commited suicide on March 1, 2007
