Victor Cruz

This site is all about Victor Cruz, one of the greatest NFL current and all time wide recievers. We will follow his journey full of hardship to the top. All his life he kept fighting to get to where he is now.


Basic Information on Cruz

  • Born on Nov. 11, 1986
  • Attended and played football at The University of Massachussets
  • Height:6'0"
  • Is currently a Wide reciever for the New York Giants

The Struggle

  • Born in Paterson, New Jersey(a rough neighborhood)
  • Attended a less known Division 1 college that doesn't recieve much recognition
  • Recieved less attention due to his size(6'0" ,204 lbs)
